Thoughts

On search engines

The world's most popular product is a search engine. Search engines have made access to information effortless.

They are the holders of true power- businesses, textbooks and feelings all need to be SEO optimized. Search result ranking has ultimately shaped society in ways we don't realize.

This made me question- do ranking algorithms have empathy? Can mom & pop corner stores compete with large corporations to rank higher?

On returns

We buy things online and consequently sometimes don't like them and return them. In the fashion industry, this can be attributed to the lack of information informing the purchase decisions- think sizing (Virtual try on trying to solve this), materials (Not all Companies are transparent) and buyer's remorse.

While ultimately eliminating returns is impossible and a loss for the consumer- there is a very important need to build tools and platforms that encourage better more informed purchase decisions.

On Career

The common advice of people telling you to do what you love is very flawed. What if I don't love work or anything else that people would pay me for?

People giving advice have to face no consequence when your career of doing things you love fails.

I'll refrain from giving out unsolicited advice. The only thing I encourage you to do- is do things. Build things, go outside, live your life. Maybe you'll find your calling. Or maybe you'll have a lot of stories.
